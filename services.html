Services

Nine Ways We Help You.

Every service we offer is designed to solve a specific financial challenge. Here is the full picture.

01
Service 01

Tax Consultancy

Tax should not be a guessing game. We work with you to understand your full income picture, identify legitimate deductions, and structure your affairs to minimise your tax liability within the bounds of Kenyan law.

Whether you are an individual, a sole proprietor, or a registered company, we build a tax strategy that fits your situation, not a template.

Who this is for
  • Business owners wanting to reduce their tax burden legally
  • Individuals with multiple income streams
  • Companies preparing for a financial audit or due diligence
Service 02

Auditing

Our independent audits examine your financial statements, internal controls, and processes to give you and your stakeholders total confidence in your numbers.

We conduct statutory audits, management audits, and donor compliance audits for NGOs and funded organisations.

Who this is for
  • Companies required to file audited accounts by law
  • NGOs with donor reporting requirements
  • Businesses seeking investor or bank financing

Service 03

Professional Bookkeeping

Messy books cost you money in overpaid tax, missed deductions, and poor decisions. We keep your records accurate, categorised correctly, and up to date so you always know where you stand.

We work with QuickBooks, Sage, Wave, and Excel-based systems depending on your preference.

Who this is for
  • SMEs without a full-time accountant
  • Businesses needing to catch up on months of backlogged records
  • Any organisation that wants clean monthly management accounts
Service 04

KRA Income Tax Reports

Income tax returns are non-negotiable, and late or incorrect filing attracts costly penalties. We prepare and submit your annual income tax returns accurately and on time, whether you are employed, self-employed, or running a company.

Who this is for
  • Employed individuals with a KRA PIN obligation
  • Self-employed professionals and consultants
  • Companies filing corporate income tax returns

Service 05

VAT and Monthly PAYE

VAT and PAYE obligations are monthly, mandatory, and unforgiving of errors. We manage your filings end-to-end: capturing the right figures, filing on the iTax portal, and sending you a summary every month so you always know what was paid.

Who this is for
  • VAT-registered businesses
  • Employers with salaried staff
  • Businesses wanting to outsource their recurring compliance entirely
Service 06

Amnesty Filing

If you are behind on KRA filings, the worst thing you can do is ignore the problem. We manage the full amnesty process: reviewing your obligations, filing all outstanding returns, engaging KRA where necessary, and restoring your compliance status cleanly.

Who this is for
  • Businesses with years of unfiled returns
  • Individuals who have never filed despite having a PIN
  • Anyone who received a KRA compliance notice

Service 07

Budgeting and Cashflow Projections

A budget you built in January is useless by March if it is not grounded in reality. We build detailed, assumption-driven budgets and rolling cashflow forecasts that actually help you make decisions month to month.

Who this is for
  • Businesses planning an expansion or new product line
  • Startups preparing for investor conversations
  • Any organisation that needs to manage cash position proactively
Service 08

Income Tax Amendments

Filed a return with errors or omissions? An unaddressed mistake compounds over time. We review your previous filings, identify the discrepancies, and handle the amendment process with KRA to correct the record and eliminate your exposure.

Who this is for
  • Individuals or businesses who suspect they overpaid tax
  • Anyone who filed returns with missing income or deductions
  • Clients who had their books redone and discovered discrepancies

Business Model Building

Whether you are launching a startup, pitching to investors, or applying for a bank loan, you need a financial model that is credible, detailed, and tells a coherent story. We build them from scratch, tailored to your specific business and audience.

Who this is for
  • Entrepreneurs launching a new venture
  • Established businesses applying for loans or grants
  • Founders preparing investor pitch decks with financial appendices
