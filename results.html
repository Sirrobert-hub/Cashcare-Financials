Case Studies

Real Clients. Real Outcomes.

Specificity builds trust. Here is a look at what working with Cashcare Financials actually delivers.

Amnesty Filing

A Nairobi-based logistics company with 23 employees

Four Years of KRA Arrears Cleared in Six Weeks

The business had not filed returns since 2020. A KRA compliance notice threatened their ability to tender for government contracts. They needed it resolved fast.

Reviewed all four years of income, VAT, and PAYE obligations
Filed all outstanding returns across all obligation types
Negotiated waiver of penalties under the amnesty window
4 Years Full compliance restored. Zero business disruption. Contract tendering resumed within 8 weeks.
Business Model Building

A family-owned restaurant group, Nairobi

KES 8M Bank Loan Approved After Two Years of Rejections

The owners had approached three banks and been rejected each time. Their financials were real and strong, but the story they told on paper was not convincing lenders.

Built a three-year financial model with full unit economics per outlet
Created scenario analysis showing conservative, base, and optimistic cases
Presented alongside a cleaned set of historical management accounts
KES 8M Loan approved within 30 days. Second branch opened six months later.
Tax and Bookkeeping

A retail chain with three branches, Central Kenya

KES 240,000 in Overpaid Tax Recovered

We were engaged to clean up their books and found a pattern of misclassified VAT input claims and a missed deduction across two financial years. The client had no idea they were owed money by KRA.

Reviewed all VAT submissions and income tax returns for 2022 and 2023
Filed income tax amendments with supporting documentation
Restructured their VAT process to prevent the same error going forward
KES 240K Recovered in overpaid tax. Client now on a monthly retainer.
