Case Studies

Real Outcomes for Real Clients.

Amnesty Filing

Logistics SME, KRA Compliance Restored

A Nairobi-based logistics company with 4 years of unfiled returns faced potential business suspension. We managed a full amnesty filing, negotiated penalty waivers, and restored full compliance status.

4yrs Backlog cleared in 6 weeks. Zero business disruption.
Business Model

Restaurant Group, Bank Financing Secured

A growing food business needed a bankable financial model to secure a KES 8M expansion loan. We built a 3-year model with scenario analysis, unit economics, and a clear narrative for the lender.

KES 8M Loan approved within 30 days of submission.
