Our Story

We Started Because You Deserve Better.

We built Cashcare Financials because we kept seeing the same thing: smart people making financial decisions without real guidance. Business owners who did not know they had overpaid KRA. Employees who had never properly filed an income tax return. Entrepreneurs who could not access a bank loan because no one had helped them build a credible financial model.

Not boilerplate advice. Actual strategy. That is what Cashcare Financials was built to deliver.

Over ten years, we have grown from a small consultancy into a trusted partner for hundreds of clients across Nairobi and beyond. We stay small enough to give you personal attention, and experienced enough to handle the most complex tax and audit situations.

We know the Kenyan tax landscape intimately: the KRA deadlines, the amnesty windows, the PAYE nuances, the audit red flags. We use that knowledge to protect you and help you grow.

Our Core Values

Integrity First

We tell you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear. Our advice is always grounded in facts, regulations, and your actual best interest.

Precision and Timeliness

Financial work demands accuracy and deadlines. We hold ourselves to the same standard we hold your books: correct, on time, every time.

Partnership Over Transaction

We do not want to be your annual accountant. We want to be the financial partner you call before making a major decision, not after.

Plain Language

We explain every recommendation in plain language. You should always understand what we are doing and why, because informed clients make better decisions.
